# Personalization

### Create custom image styles with Personalization profiles using `--p`

[![personalize-header-new.png](/hc/article_attachments/37436501709197)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/37436501709197)

V7 Personalization profiles are compatible with V8.1, and your Global Profile may need to be unlocked to use it. For more information about V8.1, see our [Version](/hc/en-us/articles/32199405667853) article.

## What is Personalization?

Personalization acts like a style assistant for your image creations. By liking or selecting images, Midjourney gets to know what kind of aesthetics you love and uses that information to generate images tailored to your personal tastes and unique style.

Personalization is compatible with Midjourney [versions](/hc/en-us/articles/32199405667853) 6, 7, and 8.1.

To create a style using a specific collection of images, check out our [Moodboards](/hc/en-us/articles/39193335040013) article.

## Unlocking Personalization

Unlocking your Global Personalization Profile is the first step to using Personalization.

**Here’s how to unlock your Global Profile:**

1. Visit the [![standard-profile-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/32434298550029)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/32434298550029) [Personalize page](https://www.midjourney.com/personalize) and find your Global V7/V8 Profile.
2. Click the thumbnail area of the profile.  
   [![unlock-profile-new.png](/hc/article_attachments/43840961844237)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/43840961844237)
3. You’ll see a grid of images. Click on the images you like the best.
4. Scroll down and keep picking your favorites.
5. As you choose, watch the progress bar move at the top of the page. Once you’ve selected enough images, your Global Profile will unlock.

[![pick-images.png](/hc/article_attachments/43840961845517)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/43840961845517)

To get the most out of your Personalization profiles, we recommend spending more time selecting images. The more images you pick, the better Midjourney gets at tailoring the images to your style.

## Using Personalization

* [On Web](#zp-1-0)
* [In Discord](#zp-1-1)

Click the Personalization[![p-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/35577114322957)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/35577114322957) button next to the Imagine bar to turn Personalization on or off (the icon will change color). Use the dropdown arrow to select your default Personalization profiles. When on, your defaults will automatically be added to all your prompts.

[![p-menu-profile.png](/hc/article_attachments/39193915292685)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/39193915292685)

You can also add `--p` to the end of your prompt in the Imagine bar to apply your default Personalization profiles to your prompt.

To use a specific Personalization profile, copy its ID from your [Personalize page](https://www.midjourney.com/personalize) with the [![use-profile-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/38800322826893)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/38800322826893) use icon and add it to the end of your prompt (example: `--p pID`). You can also use a specific code `--p code` from a previous prompt.

[![imagine-prompt-personalize-web-new.png](/hc/article_attachments/35577121023629)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/35577121023629)

Add `--p` to the end of your prompt in Discord. This will automatically apply your default Personalization profiles to your prompt.

To use a specific Personalization profile, copy its ID from your [Personalize page](https://www.midjourney.com/personalize) with the [![use-profile-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/38800322826893)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/38800322826893) use icon and add it to the end of your prompt (example: `--p pID`). You can also use a specific code `--p code` from a previous prompt.

[![discord-imagine-prompt-personalize.png](/hc/article_attachments/32433804268301)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/32433804268301)

You can also use the `🙋 Personalization` button in your Midjourney Discord [settings](/hc/en-us/articles/32868982949517) to automatically add the `--p` parameter to all your prompts.

You'll only be able to use Personalization once you've unlocked your Global Profile. If you try to use `--p` without unlocking, you'll see an error message.

## Personalization Profiles

Create and manage your Personalization profiles on the[![standard-profile-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/32434298550029)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/32434298550029) [Personalize page](https://www.midjourney.com/personalize) from the main website navigation menu.

You can create multiple Personalization profiles, each with a different style or look. Each one has a unique ID that generates a code when used in your prompts.

[![newest-personalize-page.png](/hc/article_attachments/38800322832013)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/38800322832013)

#### [global-profile-icon.svg](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/32434314881037) Global Profile

This is your default profile, and each Midjourney [version](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/32199405667853-Version) has its own Global Profile. You'll need to unlock it by selecting images before you can create additional Personalization profiles.

[![heart-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/43840969482637)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/43840969482637) Liking images from other members on the Explore page will influence your Global Profiles. Check which version the liked image was made with to see which Global Profile it will affect.

#### Additional Profiles

You can create extra Personalization profiles to focus on specific styles or different aesthetics than your Global Profile by clicking the [![restore-tool-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/38800322832781)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/38800322832781) New Profile button. New Personalization profiles will be based on your default [version](/hc/en-us/articles/32199405667853) setting, so if you want to create a new V6 Personalization profile, make sure V6 is set as your default version in your [![settings-icon.svg](/hc/article_attachments/32433728466957)](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/32433728466957) settings.

If you Rank Image Aesthetics from the [Tasks page](https://www.midjourney.com/tasks), this information will automatically apply to your Global Profile. To personalize a different one, locate it on your [Personalize page](https://www.midjourney.com/personalize) and click its name.
