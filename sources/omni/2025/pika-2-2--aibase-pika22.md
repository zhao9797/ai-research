# Pika 2.2 Officially Released: AI Video Generation Technology Receives a Major Upgrade

![aibase](https://app.aibase.com/icon/logo.png)

#### AIbase基地

Published in[AI News](/news) · 5 min read · Feb 28, 2025

570

On February 27, 2025, Pika Labs announced the launch of Pika 2.2 via its official Twitter account, @pika\_labs. This latest AI video generation model has quickly generated significant excitement among global users and tech enthusiasts. Based on user posts and feedback on Twitter, Pika 2.2 boasts several impressive upgrades, including longer video generation times, higher resolution, and innovative frame transition technology, providing users with more powerful creative tools.

[](https://upload.chinaz.com/video/2025/0228/6387632856395646729265377.mp4)

**Key Feature Highlights**

According to Twitter users, Pika 2.2 supports video generation up to 10 seconds long, a significant improvement over previous versions. Furthermore, the resolution has been boosted to 1080p, ensuring clearer and more detailed output videos. Another highly anticipated feature is "Pikaframes," a keyframe transition technology that allows users to create smoother and more creative scene transitions within a 1-to-10-second range, expanding the possibilities of video generation.

Early beta tester @taherdhanera shared on Twitter his experience with Pika 2.2's powerful features. He specifically mentioned the new version's support for cinematic ratio, resulting in extremely high-quality output and excellent prompt adherence. These features make Pika 2.2 suitable for both casual creators and professional users.

![QQ_1740703031973.png](https://upload.chinaz.com/2025/0228/6387632863616124794061352.png "QQ_1740703031973.png")

**Enthusiastic User Response**

Twitter posts show overwhelming enthusiasm for the Pika 2.2 release. Many users immediately shared the update news, including links to the official Pika website (pika.art), encouraging others to experience this "mind-blowing" AI technology. @AngryTomtweets even declared Pika 2.2 "100% AI," highlighting the high recognition of its innovative features.

**Technological Advancement and Industry Impact**

Pika Labs has steadily become an industry leader through its continuous innovation in AI video generation. From the introduction of "Scene Ingredients" in Pika 2.0, to achieving 1080p resolution and more realistic dynamic effects in Pika 2.1, and now the long-duration generation and frame transition technology in Pika 2.2, each upgrade has provided users with greater creative freedom. Twitter users generally believe that the launch of Pika 2.2 not only solidifies its position in the AI video generation market but also has the potential to significantly impact content creation, advertising production, and even the film industry.

**How to Experience It**

Users who want to try Pika 2.2 can simply visit the official website, pika.art, to start creating. As suggested by many Twitter users, the new version is now available. Whether generating personalized short videos or exploring complex scene transitions, Pika 2.2 offers unprecedented AI assistance.

With the release of Pika 2.2, AI video generation technology has undoubtedly reached a new milestone. What surprises will Pika Labs bring in the future? Let's wait and see!
