# Gemini 2.5 Flash Image (Nano Banana)

* On this page
* [Documentation](#documentation)
* [gemini-2.5-flash-image](#gemini-25-flash-image)

Our best engine for high-velocity visual creation, offering state-of-the-art
speed and efficiency. Gemini 2.5 Flash Image, also known as Nano Banana, is best
for high-volume generation, conversational image editing, and low-latency
creative workflows that require native multimodal understanding.

[Try in Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com?model=gemini-2.5-flash-image)

## Documentation

Visit the [Image generation](/gemini-api/docs/image-generation) guide for full
coverage of features and capabilities.
