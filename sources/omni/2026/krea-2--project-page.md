## Questions people ask about Krea 2

What is Krea 2?

 

Krea 2 is Krea AI's in-house AI image foundation model for generating expressive images with stronger aesthetic diversity, style references, moodboards, and creative control.

How is Krea 2 different from other AI image generators?

 

Krea 2 focuses on visual taste and style control. Instead of relying only on longer prompts, creators can use moodboards and references to guide the model toward a specific look.

What can creative teams use Krea 2 for?

 

Creative teams can use Krea 2 for campaign concepts, product imagery, brand exploration, editorial art direction, game and entertainment concepts, and fast visual iteration.

Can Krea 2 use moodboards and style references?

 

Yes. Krea 2 is designed to work with moodboards and style references, helping creators guide the model toward a specific visual language, palette, texture, or composition style.

Is Krea 2 built from scratch by Krea AI?

 

Yes. Krea 2 is Krea AI's first foundation image model built completely from scratch, with a focus on aesthetics, style transfer, and creative control.

How can businesses benefit from Krea 2?

 

Businesses can use Krea 2 to explore on-brand visuals faster, create product and campaign concepts, reduce iteration time, and test multiple creative directions before production.

When was Krea 2 announced?

 

Krea 2 was announced on May 12, 2026 as Krea's first foundation image model built completely from scratch.
