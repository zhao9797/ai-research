## Introducing Reve 2.0

 June 3, 2026

 Creativity is about choices, not guesswork.

 Today, we’re launching Reve 2.0, our most capable model yet. It debuts at #2 on Arena ’s text-to-image leaderboard, a jump of +125 Elo over v1.5, and we got there as a fraction of our competitors’ size, training on 10x fewer GPUs. For the first time, you can create images you can touch.

 Reve 2.0 generates stunning images in native 4K, built around precise layouts so you can shape and edit every part of an image instead of settling for whatever you’re handed. Whether you’re designing an ad, exploring a creative vision, or just curious about what’s possible, this model renders your ideas and then hands you the controls.

## What’s New

 Images you can touch. Every image is built from a layout: a structured map of the objects, text, and regions in a scene. That means you can reach in and change any part of it. Move a subject, rewrite the sign on the wall, or swap a background, and the rest of the scene adjusts around your edit. It’s the difference between describing a picture and directing one.

 The best 4K in the world. Every image renders in native 4K with the clarity and sharpness to hold up at full size. Zoom in on fine textures, complex scenes, and the play of light and shadow, and it stays print-ready and in focus.

 Text and references that hold up. Signs, labels, posters, and logos come out clean and legible where other models stumble. Stack more reference images than ever into a single composition to lock in a style, a character, or a look, and watch people render with real range and accuracy.

 A rebuilt editor. Select any object and its controls appear right away. Results stream in as the model works, and the canvas stays front and center, so you spend your time creating, not hunting through panels.
