Announcement

[### Draft mode for V8.1 and new feature previews](/updates/draft-mode-for-v8-1-and-new-feature-previews)

Hi everyone - a few updates today!

**Draft mode for V8.1**

* Each draft mode generation creates 24 images at a lower resolution and quality.
* Click "Vary" on images you'd like to render at full-quality and full-resolution
* Draft jobs use half as many fast hours as V8.1 SD jobs (even though they generate 24 images!)
* Turn on draft mode by clicking the ⚡ button in the menubar

![](https://storage.ghost.io/c/95/61/95616ddb-b827-4269-ad22-ff4a6b368a1f/content/images/2026/06/Screenshot-2026-06-16-at-2.53.46---PM.jpeg)

**Use --preview to test new features**

* We're trying something new today to let you test early versions of new models. Use `--preview` in your prompt to test the latest. Images that you create with `--preview` might be a little unpolished and the jobs are not guaranteed to run consistently over time.
* In this first test, you'll notice differences across all images, especially those with personalization and moodboards.

We'd love to hear what you think in [#ideas-and-features](https://discord.com/channels/662267976984297473/989270517401911316)

[### V8.1 is now the default model on Midjourney!](/updates/v8-1-is-now-the-default-model)

Hi everyone,

After your testing and feedback, we've updated the default model from V7 to V8.1!

**A reminder of what's new in V8.1:**

* The model is smarter, more coherent, better adheres to detailed prompts, and renders text better than ever
* With HD mode enabled, V8.1 renders at twice the size and 4x the resolution of V7 images

[### Web Updates](/updates/web-updates-5)

Conversational mode has been improved across text and voice input:

* Prompt scrolling on the Create page no longer traps the scroll wheel.
* Subscribe button no longer gets stuck after navigating back.
* Activating a Niji 6 or V6 Personalization profile no longer immediately deselects it.
* Copy confirmation now waits for the clipboard write to finish.
* Corrections for inaccuracies in the Account Settings subscription status panel.

* Obviously we can't guarantee this but it's a big cool goal and worth our time to give it a shot!

[March 18, 2026New](/updates/v8-alpha)

5:08 AM

Announcement

[### V8 Alpha](/updates/v8-alpha)

![](https://storage.ghost.io/c/95/61/95616ddb-b827-4269-ad22-ff4a6b368a1f/content/images/2026/03/image.png)

Hi everyone! Today we’re letting the community test an early version of our V8 model on [alpha.midjourney.com](https://alpha.midjourney.com). We want your feedback and your help. Let’s explore what this new model can do.

**V8 is an incredible model.** It’s much better at following detailed directions and will still surprise you when you ask. Its ability to understand your aesthetics through personalization, style references (srefs) and moodboards is amazing. Images are more coherent and detailed. Text rendering works better than ever (when specified in “quotes”). And image generation is ~5x faster than before.

We’ve **upgraded our web interfaces** to support this speed. And more is coming. We have an improved conversation mode so you can just ‘talk’ in flow, a “Grid Mode” so you can focus on a single big set of images, and we’ve moved our settings into ‘sidebars’ so you can tweak settings without blocking your view.

V8 launches with support for multiple aspect ratios, **`--chaos`, `--weird`, `--exp`, `--raw`**. We support **backwards compatibility** with your V7 personalization profiles, moodboards, and srefs.

V8 brings a new **`--hd` mode** which natively renders images at 2K resolution and a **`--q 4` mode** if you need a little bit of extra coherence.

**Modes and Pricing:** Presently, Relax is not yet supported. We are working on a new server cluster for Relax plus cheaper render modes. `--hd`, `--q 4`, sref, and Moodboard jobs are 4x as slow as regular jobs and each costs 4x (for now).

**The best way to help us improve V8** is to rate images in the lightbox. Click an image in create then click “I like this image” or “I don't like this image” on as many of your V8 images as possible. You can use 1/2/3 hotkeys and arrow keys to rate images quickly.

**Other Tips and Tricks:** If you’re going for photos or a more ‘plain’ or ‘in control’ look we’d recommend switching to `--raw` right away or using moodboards or srefs to control your style. We’re still working on the ‘default’ aesthetics of V8, but we strongly encourage you to use your personalization and even crank it to –stylize 1000 asap. V8 is going to shine most right now when you rely heavily on our stylization systems and when you trend towards longer more specific prompting.

**Please Note**: This is an entirely new model with unique strengths and weaknesses, we want to learn from you what it's good and bad at but keep in mind it may require totally new prompting styles. So play around a bit!

It’s time to play! Show off what you’re making and let us know what you think on [Discord](https://discord.gg/midjourney). As the model becomes more mature, we’ll do a community-wide roadmap ranking session to help us figure out what to prioritize next.

Thanks again for everyone’s help with the V8 pre-release rating parties, and thank you so much for being a part of Midjourney! Let’s have fun out there and find new wonders on this vast and shared sea of imagination.

Change Log

[### An update on Rooms](/updates/an-update-on-rooms)

Today we are removing the Rooms feature from the website.

Rooms was an experiment that taught us a lot about what our community needs and where our current infrastructure falls short. It tried to solve too many things at once, and didn't do any of it well enough. Rather than continue patching something that wasn't built to scale, we're making space to build these experiences properly.

We know this leaves some gaps, mainly around live support for our web users and a place for live events or collaboration. We’re actively working on filling those gaps with products focused on each use case. We'll share updates when we have them.

Moving forward, if you need support, you can:

* Visit our [help page](https://www.midjourney.com/help) for immediate assistance
* Join [#support](https://discord.com/channels/662267976984297473/958069758211797092) or [#promptcraft](https://discord.com/channels/662267976984297473/992207085146222713) on Discord for live help
* Email [support@midjourney.com](mailto:support@midjourney.com)

Thank you to everyone who shared something inside Rooms. We’ll bring them back in a better form.

[### HD mode for video](/updates/hd-mode-for-video)

Heya everyone, we're releasing a HD mode for our video model today!

[

](https://storage.ghost.io/c/95/61/95616ddb-b827-4269-ad22-ff4a6b368a1f/content/media/2025/08/hd-lion.mp4)


**About HD Video Mode**

* This HD mode is very expensive to run and we've increased the cost proportionally. It is ~3.2x more expensive than normal Midjourney video.
* HD mode is for professionals with higher budgets who don't mind paying the cost for the highest possible video quality
* HD mode is only available on Pro and Mega plans and does not work with relax mode
* HD mode will render at native 720p resolution. Like our normal SD resolution video model, we think our quality at each resolution is outstanding
* The higher resolution will reduce small scale pixel artifacts and may give you slightly better coherence or motion
* Many professionals report no longer wanting to use post-process upscalers when using HD mode.

**Please note:** We think if you're making videos just for fun, it is not necessary or desirable to use HD mode due to the additional cost.

**How to enable HD Video mode**

* You can enable HD mode on the website by clicking the settings button on the right side of the prompt bar then clicking "HD" under "Video Resolution"
